# Local Signal Room Page Overrides

## Intent

Create a calm infrastructure canvas for MakerLab growth operations. Take the interaction quality from Railway: compact product navigation, a strong operational thesis, a visual system map, clear states, and progressive detail. Do not copy Railway branding, copy, purple palette, logos, or cloud-product metaphors.

## Visual direction

- **Mode:** Dark, local-only workspace; use deep ink and blue-black surfaces rather than a white dashboard.
- **MakerLab signature:** Acid lime `#C5F36A` for the single decisive action and active state, warm orange `#F18A51` only for caution, cyan `#77D8EC` for system/signal detail, and mint `#71E0AF` for ready states.
- **Hero:** Two panels. The left side makes one clear promise; the right side shows the agent architecture as a useful canvas, not decoration.
- **Navigation:** A compact desktop command bar. On mobile, retain four labelled tabs in a bottom dock and keep the first action above the dock.
- **Density:** Keep operational information dense but give every number a label and a truthful state. Never create fake performance data.

## Motion and accessibility

- Animate only the central command node with a slow pulse; all other movement should be hover/focus feedback (180-250ms).
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` by disabling the pulse.
- Preserve 44px touch controls, strong focus outlines, text labels alongside every status color, and no mobile horizontal scrolling.

## Anti-patterns

- Do not return to a light card grid with repeated rounded white boxes.
- Do not hide the next recommended action behind an unlabeled icon.
- Do not make the visual architecture imply that analytics are live when sources are not connected.
